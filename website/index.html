Built for how you actually play

One round. Every game. No spreadsheet.

Most apps make you pick one format and stick to it. Real golf isn't like that — you've got a Nassau, a skins game, and three side bets all running at once. Halved tracks all of it.

Any format, all at once

Run a Nassau, skins, Wolf, and presses on the same round. Halved keeps every game separate and scores them all from one set of inputs.

Live the whole way

See exactly where you stand on every tee box. Know when you're down a skin and need to press — while there's still holes left to play.

Just enter scores

No more cluttered cards and mental math. Tap in your scores like normal and let Halved handle the handicaps, the carries, and the running totals.

Run a full tournament

From a weekend foursome to a 60-player outing. Live leaderboards, multiple groups, every format — follow the whole field as it unfolds.

Stats that follow you

Head-to-head records, win streaks, and how you really do against your buddies — round after round, season after season.

Settled, not argued

The round ends with one clean summary of who owes whom. No disputes, no awkward Venmo math on the 19th hole.

How it works

Three taps from first tee to settled up.

1

Set the games

Pick your formats and stakes, add your group with a code or link. Followers can watch without even making an account.

2

Play & enter scores

Just put in your numbers as you go. Halved scores every game live, applies handicaps, and tracks the action hole by hole.

3

Settle up

On the 18th, get one tidy breakdown of who owes whom across every game. Square up however you already do.

The best part

The argument ends before it starts.

Every game on the round, totaled and netted into one number per person. No "wait, did the press carry?" No paper card covered in scribbles.

Just a clean, shareable summary you can drop in the group chat the second you walk off the green.

Halved for clubs

Run your whole club's action — not just a foursome.

Member-guest tournaments, season-long match-play ladders, club championships. Unlimited members, unlimited flights, every format your members actually play — with a live club-wide leaderboard following all of it. Halved sits alongside your existing tools, not on top of them.

No format is off-limits
Built because the presets in other apps were too rigid. Combine and customize games however your members play.
Unlimited flights & members
From a 16-player member-guest to a full-field club championship across multiple groups.
Live club-wide leaderboard
The whole field, every game, updating in real time — on phones and on the clubhouse TV.
An organizer console
Your pro or committee sets up events, manages rosters and settles the field — without a spreadsheet.

Works alongside the tools your club already runs

Halved is complementary, not a rip-and-replace. It's designed to fit alongside the systems you already use — your rosters, your sign-on, and your members' handicaps for accurate net scoring — so there's nothing to rip out. Halved is the member-facing scoring layer on top.
