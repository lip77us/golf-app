Halved
Track golf bets with your group — right from your phone.
Get the app

Halved is a mobile app for golfers who play friendly games for stakes. Set up a casual round, pick your games — skins, Nassau, points, Sixes, match play and more — and Halved handles the handicaps, the scoring, and who owes whom at the end. No more napkin math on the 18th green.

What you can do

Set up a round

Add your players, pick a course and tees, and choose the games you're playing for the day.

Score as you go

Enter scores hole by hole; Halved computes nets, presses, skins, and points automatically.

Settle up

A clear leaderboard shows standings and who owes what — so settling up is simple and friendly.
